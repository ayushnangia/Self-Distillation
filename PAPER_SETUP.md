# Paper Training Setup (arXiv:2601.19897)

Extracted from the actual paper LaTeX source (`paper/arxiv.tex`). Use this as ground truth for all experiments.

## Key Facts

- **Model**: Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct (all experiments)
- **Hardware**: Single NVIDIA H200
- **Seeds**: 3 random seeds per config, report mean + 95% CI
- **Selection**: Best VALIDATION checkpoint on target task (not last, not highest test)
- **SDFT cost**: ~2.5x FLOPs, ~4x wall-clock vs SFT
- **Library**: HuggingFace TRL
- **Fine-tuning**: Full (no LoRA/PEFT)

## Datasets

- **Science Q&A**: SciKnowEval Chemistry L-3 subset (Feng et al.). Paper: "approximately 75% train, 5% validation, 20% test." Our actual split: 1800 train, 120 val, 480 test (2400 total). Demos generated by GPT-4o (up to 8 samples per prompt, keep 1 correct). Accuracy by exact match on answer letter.
- **Tool Use**: ToolAlpaca (Tang et al.), original train-test split. Our data: 4046 train, 97 test (Arrow). Paper eval uses 68-example subset (`eval_data_paper_68.json`). Demos included in original dataset. Accuracy by regex matching against ground-truth API call.
- **Medical**: HuatuoGPT-o1 English subset (Chen et al.). Paper: "approximately 20,000 examples" for training, "1,000 randomly sampled verifiable questions" for eval. Our actual split: 18204 train, 500 val, 1000 test (19704 total from HF dataset). Eval by GPT-5-mini judge (CORRECT/INCORRECT).
- **Knowledge Acquisition**: 9 Wikipedia articles on 2025 natural disasters (post-cutoff). ~100 QA pairs per article generated by GPT-5 (following Mecklenburg et al.).

## Evaluation Protocol

- **Greedy accuracy**: temperature=0
- **pass@k**: temperature=1.0, top_p=0.95, k up to 128
- **Benchmarks**: HellaSwag, TruthfulQA, MMLU, IFEval, Winogrande, HumanEval (via lm-evaluation-harness)
- **Medical judge**: GPT-5-mini, outputs CORRECT/INCORRECT
- **3 seeds**, mean + 95% CI

- Task order: Science → Tool Use → Medical
- Single model trained sequentially on each task
- Performance linearly normalized (0=base, 1=max)

### Key Discrepancies

1. **Base tool-use accuracy**: Our 52.9% vs paper 42.9% (+10pp). Same 68-example eval set. May reflect different generation settings (our max_model_len=2048, seed=0).
2. **HellaSwag**: Our base=65.3 vs paper=62.0. We report acc_norm at 0-shot; paper may use different metric or n-shot setting.
3. **Winogrande**: Our base=60.3 vs paper=71.1 (−11pp). Large gap suggests different lm-eval task config or version.
4. **SDFT-1000 forgetting**: Massive degradation (MMLU 52.1, IFEval 45.1) — this model was trained ~8 epochs at lr=2e-5 (not in paper's grid), far past optimal.
5. **SFT HellaSwag anomaly**: Our SFT=76.4 vs paper=57.3 (+19pp). Suspiciously high — needs investigation.
6. **HumanEval missing**: Paper includes HumanEval in Avg Prior (6 benchmarks); our avg is over 5.

### Root Causes to Investigate

- **lm-eval version/config mismatch**: Our benchmarks use 0-shot, no few-shot examples. Paper may use different settings. Winogrande and HellaSwag gaps suggest this.
- **No hyperparameter sweep**: Paper picks best from 54+ configs × 3 seeds. We evaluate single checkpoints from HF Hub.
- **SDFT checkpoints are not paper's best**: step-100 and step-1000 are intermediate checkpoints, not the paper's best-validated model.
- **Need to re-run with paper's exact lm-eval config** once we determine their n-shot settings and task versions.
